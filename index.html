objective Your objective in this game is to find matching triplets of images by flipping the cards. Each correct match will earn you a point. The more points you score, the better!

gamesetup 1.There are a total of 10 different images in the container, with each image appearing 3 times. That makes a total of 30 cards for you to flip.

gameplay 1.To flip a card, simply click on it. Clicking on a card will reveal the image on the other side. Remember, each click counts as one flip.
2.You can click on two more cards to reveal their images as well, trying to find three cards with the same image.
3.When you successfully find three cards with matching images, all three cards will be removed from the container, and you will earn one point.
4.If the three cards you flipped don't match, they will be flipped back face down.
5.Keep flipping cards to find more matching triplets. Pay attention to the cards you've already flipped to improve your chances of finding matches.

scoring Each correct match (finding a triplet of identical images) will earn you one point.

timer The timer will start counting as soon as you begin the game and will continue running until you decide to end the game.
You will see the elapsed time in seconds, minutes, or hours, depending on how long you take to complete the game.

restarting After the game ends, you can choose to play again..
Get ready to test your memory and matching skills in the Cards Flip Game. Have fun, and let the flipping begin!
